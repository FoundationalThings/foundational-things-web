MonoNote

It's like a sticky note for your mobile device.

Using widgets, you can place virtual sticky notes on every screen you choose and your note will appear on all of them. MonoNote is especially helpful for that one thing you need to remember.

This mobile app has just entered what I call the Ultra Beta stage. MonoNote is exclusively available to folks who show great interest, or who get volun-told to use the app.

Once we work out a few growing pains, the project will enter a more publicly available pre-release Beta period.

If you're curious to learn more about MonoNote, or interested supporting this work as a product tester, please reach out.

// About Donna

I'm Donna Harris, the real-life person behind Foundational Things. My work lives at the intersection of people and technology, building software and learning experiences that are both useful and human.

As a solopreneur, I bring together a multi-faceted background in software development, deeply held personal and professional values, and the practical support of AI tools available in 2026. I use all of it to create thoughtful, grounded technology experiences.

In my work as a business analyst, I learned to repeatedly ask and respond to a simple but powerful question: What problem are we trying to solve? I also learned that it is surprisingly easy to build something impressive that doesn't truly address any real need.

Over time, I began noticing problems in my own life that I wanted to solve more intentionally. That curiosity led me to explore how software and systems could better support everyday challenges. I've found that others are navigating similar challenges, which is why I want to share and grow products publicly under the Foundational Things brand.

I'm also an educator, musician, and lifelong learner. I have a teacher's heart and care deeply about helping people grow more confident with technology. When I'm not building software, I create resources that help people better understand technology, both how it's built and how to use it well in everyday life.
