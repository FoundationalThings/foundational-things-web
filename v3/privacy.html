// Privacy Policy

Even though this isn't very official looking yet, please know that your online safety, privacy, and data matter to us!

We desire to be transparent, fair, and open about what we do here. So, as we figure out what all really belongs on this page, we will update our policies with intention and clarity because you deserve to know what's going on and how it impacts you.

Here are things we can tell you right up front:

We don't sell data...

But we are likely sharing some data.

We use a number of free services to run Foundational Things. The use of free services often means paying with data in some way.

We do keep some info...

If you provide us with your name and contact information, we will hang on to it... unless you ask us not to.

Whenever you give us this information, there will be a reasonable possibility that we will want or need to follow up with you. As these situations arise, we will clearly state what information we're keeping and why. We'll also give details on how to opt out, if you choose.

We keep it for our use and records only and will not knowingly provide it to anyone else without your consent.

Paying Foundational Things

If you provide funds to Foundational Things, for any reason, here's what you need to know:

We use Ko-Fi and Stripe, both as free services.

Ko-Fi is a platform that exists to help creators like us to get support from people like you! It handles donations, subscriptions, and selling products for us. You can read their privacy policy here.

Our Ko-Fi page is set up to use Stripe for payments, such as product purchases and tips/donations. You might also see reference to one of Stripe's products, Link, which is used for the actual processing. You can read Stripe's and Link's privacy policies.

We don't keep your payment details.

We will never keep any payment card or account details stored. If for some reason we are given access to this information, we will only use it with your consent and we will destroy our copy of the information after its use.

We use Cloudflare

Cloudflare is a globally trusted security service. They keep our web products and sites healthy and secure, which keeps you protected and gives the best experience possible. Cloudflare will collect information about your visits, but only as it needs to fulfil its security responsibilities.

They do not sell, rent, or share any of your personal information. You can read their privacy policy here.
